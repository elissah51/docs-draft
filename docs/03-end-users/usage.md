# End Users Guide

Welcome to the Quanterra platform. This section is designed for **end users** who interact directly with the system. Follow this guide to get started and make the most of the platform.

## Who are End Users?

End users are individuals who use the platform to perform their daily tasks, track information, and access resources. They do **not** manage administrative settings or organizational configurations.

## Key Capabilities

- Access and complete assigned tasks or forms  
- View personal dashboards and progress reports  
- Receive notifications and updates relevant to their account  
- Customize personal preferences and settings

## Getting Started

1. **Login**  
   - Navigate to the platform login page  
   - Enter your credentials provided by your organization  

2. **Dashboard Overview**  
   - Upon login, the dashboard displays your current tasks, notifications, and recent activity  

3. **Completing Tasks**  
   - Select a task from your dashboard  
   - Follow the instructions and submit your responses  

4. **Profile Settings**  
   - Click on your profile icon to update personal information, password, and preferences  

## Tips for Optimal Use

- Keep your profile information up to date  
- Check notifications regularly to stay informed  
- Contact support if you encounter any issues
